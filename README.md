## Project Overview
The **Case Management & Auto-Escalation ‚Äì Service Cloud** project automates customer service operations for faster and more efficient case resolution. Cases are intelligently routed and escalated based on priority, SLA, and customer type, minimizing manual effort and ensuring timely responses.

**Key Features:**
- Automated **case assignment** with **Flows** and **Assignment Rules**  
- SLA-based **Escalation Rules** and **Approval Processes**  
- **Apex Triggers** & **Asynchronous Apex** for complex escalation logic  
- Integration with **email** and **chat support systems**  
- Real-time monitoring via **Reports** and **Dashboards**  

---

## Objectives
1. Automate case routing and escalation to reduce resolution time  
2. Ensure SLA compliance for high-priority or overdue cases  
3. Reduce manual workload and improve operational efficiency  
4. Integrate external communication channels for seamless customer support  
5. Provide visibility into agent performance and case metrics

- **Salesforce Platform:** Service Cloud, Case, Account, Contact, Reports, Dashboards
